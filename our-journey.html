Our Journey

How Akoya began.

I will never forget the moment it began.

During a needle localization, a patient suddenly lost consciousness and required immediate assistance. Working alongside the lead technologist, I stepped in to help support her as she began to slide from the chair, ensuring her safety in a critical moment. That experience stayed with me—and ultimately became the catalyst for Akoya Medical.

Over the next several years, my partner and I examined how patient distress during interventional breast procedures could be reduced. We identified an overlooked factor—visual exposure—and developed patented eyewear designed to block the lower field of vision during biopsy procedures.

Vasovagal syncope is a well-recognized response that can compromise patient safety, comfort, and workflow. By limiting visual stimuli, Akoya eyewear is designed to promote a calmer, more controlled experience for both patients and clinical staff.

We hope these samples help support improved outcomes and a more positive experience for your patients.

— Drew Zaun, Co-founder, Akoya Medical
